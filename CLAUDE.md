# Blue Collar Dogs

> Website redesign for Boulder-based dog training business

---

## Client

**Blue Collar Dogs** - Dog training and behavioral services in Boulder, CO
- Owner/Trainer: Zane Marsh
- Current site: https://blucollardogs.com/

## Project Scope

Simple, clean static website that honors the core brand:
- **Tagline**: "A Working Dog is a Happy Dog"
- **Philosophy**: Natural dog psychology, relationship-focused training
- **Voice**: Professional yet approachable, patient, educational

## Services to Feature

1. Puppy & Introductory Training - $450 (4 sessions)
2. General Handling & Understanding Dogs - $600
3. Advanced Obedience - $175/session
4. Behavioral Transformation Package - $900
5. Pack Walks - $50 (90 min)
6. Individual Dog Walks - $40/hour
7. Drop-In Visits - $40/hour

## Tech Stack

- Simple static HTML/CSS
- Mobile-responsive
- Fast, accessible
- No build tools needed

## Brand Values

- Dogs thrive with purpose and engagement
- Patience, consistency, timeliness, objectivity
- Transforming challenges into growth opportunities
- Creating healthy relationships between handlers and dogs
