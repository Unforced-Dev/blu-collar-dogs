Hey, I'm Zane

I studied psychology and neuroscience, then learned the practical side from a Schutzhund trainer. The two go together better than you'd think.

I don't believe in "bad dogs." Most problems come down to confusion—the dog doesn't know what's expected, or the human doesn't have the right tools yet. We fix both.

My approach is balanced. Not purely positive, not heavy-handed. Clear, fair, and focused on building a real relationship between you and your dog.

"Train with your dog, not to your dog."

What I Help With

Reactive Dogs

Lunging, barking, losing it at other dogs or people. Walks don't have to feel like a battle.

Anxious Dogs

Fearful, shut down, overwhelmed. We build confidence at their pace, not ours.

The Basics

Pulling, jumping, ignoring you. Sometimes you just need the right approach.
