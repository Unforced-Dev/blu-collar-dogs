Meet Your Trainer

Zane Marsh

I don't believe in "bad dogs." I believe in confused dogs with handlers who haven't been given the right tools yet.

With a degree in psychology, a minor in neuroscience, and hands-on training from both a clinical psychologist and a Schutzhund dog trainer, I bridge the gap between understanding why dogs behave the way they do and how to actually change it.

My approach is balanced—not purely positive, not punishment-based. I meet each dog where they are and create a clear path forward. No gimmicks. No shortcuts. Just honest work that gets results.

"Train with your dog, not to your dog. That shift changes everything."
